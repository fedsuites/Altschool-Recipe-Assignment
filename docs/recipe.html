Spaghetti Bolognese

This classic Italian pasta dish combines rich, savory flavors of meat, tomatoes, and herbs, creating a comforting and hearty meal perfect for family dinners.

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 300g ground beef
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© onion until translucent.
  3. Add garlic and ground beef, cooking until browned.
  4. Stir in canned tomatoes and oregano; let simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve sauce over spaghetti.

Tips

For a richer flavor, add a splash of red wine when simmering the sauce. You can also top with freshly grated Parmesan cheese!
